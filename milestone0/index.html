Welcome

Hello,

Thank you for choosing our technology.

To start, please follow these steps:

1. Connect devices (air conditioner controllers, On/Off controller and power meters) manual
2. Create app account/s on Android or iOS App (search Airconet) download
3. Link devices to App in each location. Give each device a recognizable name app setup

After all devices can work with App in all locations, follow these steps:

4. Create sub accounts according to your organization chart example
5. Give each account login username and password and mark who is responsible for cloud’s fees
6. Link the App accounts in groups according to its location in the organization (in Level 3) using
the email and password used to sign in the App
7. Go to ‘Control’ page and ensure all devices can work
8. Setup the others features such as Timers, Holiday, Alerts, etc.

Note: you have 1 free month to test the system. Please add your payment method before the expiry date to ensure the system continues to work

You can always come back to this QuickStart by pressing the ‘i’ on the top right corner in Setup page
